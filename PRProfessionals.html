Exhibitor/Sponsor PR Professionals

CVPR 2026 is offering a complimentary PR program for exhibiting and sponsoring companies. This initiative is designed to help you maximize your visibility and connect effectively with the global media covering the event.

Program Benefits

Participating companies will receive support through several key channels:

  • Media Promotion: Promotion of planned events and announcements to registered media in pre-conference email updates.
  • Press Release Sharing: Sharing of exhibitor and sponsor press releases in on-site email communications to media invitees and registrants.
  • Media Center Access: Opportunities to schedule media events and meetings in the on-site Media Center at CVPR 2026.
  • Media Lists: Access to the official pre-conference media list.
  • And more.

To participate in the program, please have your company’s PR representative complete the registration form. From there, the CVPR Media Team will reach out to confirm next steps. We look forward to working with you!
