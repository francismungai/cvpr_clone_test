Registration Requirements

  • Author Requirements: Each paper (Main Conference AND Workshop) MUST be registered under an AUTHOR registration. (There is a student rate under AUTHOR). One registration may cover up to three papers inclusive of main conference or workshop papers. Additional papers will have to be registered under a separate registration.
  • Virtual Presenters: Virtual registrations will not cover a paper submission - even workshop papers. If circumstances prevent you from attending in person to present your paper, you may attend virtually but you must register and pay for your paper under an author registration and select that you will attend virtually.

What's Included?

Full Passport and AUTHOR Registrations: Includes admission to all technical sessions, all tutorials, all workshops, any catered functions, and online access to the proceedings as well as the virtual platform.

Virtual Registrations: Includes admission to all online technical sessions, recorded tutorials, recorded workshops, and online access to the proceedings.

Payment & Refund Policy

Payment/Invoice: The only acceptable payment is via credit card through the registration site. We will not accept wire payment, check or PO. A receipt will be issued upon completion. Please use the address needed for your receipt during registration. There will be a fee for any changes to payments. If you need a refund due to using the wrong card, a $25 fee will be associated with the change.

Refunds: All refund requests must be made via email to Nicole Finn. All cancellations will be subject to a $65 cancellation fee before May 13, 2026, 11:59pm MT. NO REFUNDS will be made after May 13, 2026. You are allowed to change the name on the registration without penalty by logging into your registration.

Visa Letter Request

After you have registered, you should receive an email within 5 minutes with instructions on how to get an invitation letter. If you don't receive the email:

  • Within 5 minutes, a matching profile at CVF Conference Website will be created if one doesn't already exist. Use the password reset page to set your password if needed.
  • Visit the invitation letter page to generate your invitation letter. The letter will be emailed to you. We do not store your passport number or birth date.
